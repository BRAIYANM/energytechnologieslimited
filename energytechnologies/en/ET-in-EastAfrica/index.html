Proyectos destacados

Energy Technologies is a company specialising in large infrastructure construction and concessions in the areas of energy, civil engineering, environment and facilities. It has a track record of over 80 years' experience, operating in more than 40 countries on four continents.

The diversification of its activity and the determined focus on increasing it international market are key elements in Energy Technologies growth strategy. The company has a multicultural and multilingual staff of over 6,000 professionals from 43 different nationalities.

The Group is a benchmark world-wide in the high-voltage power distribution market, with more than 3,000 kilometers of lines under concession in Brazil and India.

The company is also an international benchmark in building EPC photovoltaic plants, with an installed capacity built of more than 750 MW. Apart from its PV business, the group plays a vital role in the construction and commissioning of all kinds of electrical generation plants: open cycle, combined cycle, cogeneration, coal-fired, wind-driven, photovoltaic, biomass and hydraulic.

In all the countries where we operate, Isolux Corsán's activities are driven by our commitment to the environment and to sustainability, as well as respect towards prevailing laws everywhere.

Description

The project was awarded by the National Electric Energy Agency. This contract also includes the associated substation (530/345 kV and 500/138 kV, 1,800 MVA of transformation).

This transmission line and substations correspond to the existing Cachoeira Paulista complex, which is being managed by Energy Technologies. The development of this important project, with an investment of 13 M, has led to the creation of about 1,900 jobs. Contact
